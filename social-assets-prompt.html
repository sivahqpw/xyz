Reusable Social Media Assets Prompt

Act as my ethical social media marketing copywriter. 
I want you to write the following four elements for my [niche/topic/offer] that I am promoting on [specific social media platform]:

1) A clear, audience-specific **Profile Bio** that:
   - Calls out the target audience and their main pain point.
   - Mentions the free lead magnet I’m offering.
   - Tells them exactly what to DM:  DM me “[keyword]” and I’ll send it to you right now.
   - Maintains an ethical tone with no misleading claims.

2) A **Pinned Post** for my personal profile that:
   - Hooks attention with a relatable headline.
   - Describes the value or result the audience wants.
   - Introduces the free lead magnet in 2–3 bullet points.
   - Gives a clear CTA to DM a keyword: DM me “[keyword]” and I’ll send it to you right now.
   - Uses clear, compliant, and professional language.

3) A **Dedicated Promotional Post** for my feed that:
   - Builds interest by sharing a short story, insight, or pain point.
   - Highlights what makes this lead magnet unique and easy to use.
   - Clearly says what problem it solves for the audience.
   - Gives an easy CTA to DM a keyword or comment to receive it.

4) A **Pinned Post** for my private group dedicated to this lead magnet that:
   - Welcomes new members and reminds them why they’re here.
   - Clearly explains how to access or download the lead magnet.
   - Sets expectations for engagement (e.g., introduce yourself, ask questions).
   - Repeats the lead magnet’s benefit and ethical promise.

Always use simple, clear language that is legally safe (no false claims, no guarantees if not proven). Always respect international marketing standards and platform guidelines.

Use this exact structure and mindset every time. 
[Include any extra context, like what the lead magnet is, who it’s for, and your brand’s personality if needed.]
